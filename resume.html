Hashtag2Action: Data Engineering and Self-Supervised Pre-Training for Action Recognition in Short-Form Videos

Yang Qian, Ali Kargarandehkordi, Yinan Sun, Parnian Azizian, Onur Cezmi Mutlu, Saimourya Surabhi, Zain Jabbar, Dennis Wall, Peter Washington, Huaijin Chen

Proceedings of the IEEE/CVF International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV) Workshops, 2025

Short-form social-media videos offer a rich, low-cost source of behavioural data, yet their weak or noisy labels and privacy constraints complicate direct use in action recognition. We introduce Hashtag2Action (H2A), a scalable pipeline that transforms raw TikTok clips into a 283k-clip dataset spanning 386 action categories. The model achieves top-1 accuracies of 99.1% (UCF101), 86.1% (HMDB51), 85.5% (Kinetics-400), and 74.3% (SSv2) with only 20% of VideoMAE V2â€™s pretraining data.
